Ambulance at Your Doorstep, Within Minutes.

In moments of crisis, every second matters. Emcare brings you immediate access to verified ambulances and trained responders — because your loved ones deserve nothing but the fastest care.

Why Emcare?

When every second can mean the difference between life and death, you can’t afford delays, confusion, or unverified services.Emcare is a network of certified ambulance providers, trained drivers, and real-time tracking, designed to give you peace of mind in the worst moments. We don’t just book ambulances — we coordinate rapid medical response across cities and towns in India. With smart routing, transparent pricing, and 24x7 support, Emcare brings speed, safety, and reliability to emergency medical transport.

How It Works

Using Emcare is as simple as it gets — and that’s by design. Just open the app or website, enter your location, select the type of ambulance you need (Basic Life Support, Advanced Life Support, etc.), and hit Book Now. We’ll instantly show you the nearest available ambulances, along with their estimated arrival time, price, and live location. Need help? Our 24/7 helpline and chat support is just one tap away. Because in emergencies, you shouldn’t have to think — you should just get help. Fast. Smart. Seamless.
